  1. Pelican TOC Plugin

    Fri 16 October 2015
    By Wang YanJiong

    插件准备

    • 使用 extract_toc

    • 执行git clone https://github.com/getpelican/pelican-plugins.git

    修改配置文件

    • 文件：pelicanconf.py

    • 指定插件目录

    PLUGIN_PATHS = ['<your_path>/getpelican/pelican-plugins']

    • 启用插件

    PLUGINS = ['extract_toc']

    Markdown标记

    • 插入[TOC]标签，例如：
        title: Pelican TOC Plugin
    date: 2015-10-16
    modified: 2015-10-17
    tags: pelican
    category: pelican
